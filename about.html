About Me

I'm Andrei Arroyo, a certified personal trainer dedicated to helping you become the best version of yourself. With over five years of experience in the fitness industry, I specialize in pushing individuals beyond their limits through a unique approach that combines science-based techniques with personalized coaching.


Growing up being the "fat kid", I know what it's like firsthand to go through a complete physical transformation. I discovered the formula that will always work: patience combined with consistency.

Whether you're a beginner taking your first steps into fitness or an athlete aiming for new heights, I provide the motivation, guidance, and accountability needed to achieve your goals.


I founded Push Past Limits Coaching as a way to share my knowledge and experience with the world.
